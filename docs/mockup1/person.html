Marissa

Why am I homeless?

After surviving abuse, Marissa was dropped from foster care after she discovered she was pregnant. She lived in hotels and couch surfed until she lost her car.

Did you know... 10% of homeless people aged out of foster care and could not sustain a home without a support network.

30% are women

Where am I from?

Nearly 70% of the homeless have lived in LA for over 20 years.

What do I do?

Marissa has three part-time jobs to make a better life for her children. She works for a delivery service, as a cafeteria assistant and at a gas station.

Where am I now?

Marissa currently lives in a homeless shelter with her four kids.
